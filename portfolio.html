1.3.1 final project

I worked with Katie,Maan and Alec to create a version of the game Flappy Bird. We allowed the user to move the bird with the keys on the keyboard. The point of the game is move the bird through the pipes. By doing this you get a score for every pipe you go through. At the end a leaderboard is displayed with the high score and the players score. The pipes randomly generate in height onto the screen. When the bird hits the pipes (collison) the bird falls and that is the end of the game.
